CalmTouch Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Jan 6, 2026

Information We Collect

CalmTouch stores user-generated information such as mood check-ins, journal entries, session history, preferences, goals, and cognitive offload entries locally on your device. CalmTouch does not operate its own servers to collect or store personal data.

CalmTouch does not require account creation, and we do not associate user data with any personal identifiers.

⸻

How We Use Your Information

The information stored within CalmTouch is used solely to enable app functionality, including progress tracking, streaks, session recommendations, journaling, and preserving user preferences. All processing occurs on your device.

⸻

Data Storage

User data is stored locally using your device’s secure storage mechanisms. CalmTouch does not upload or sync user data to external servers or cloud services controlled by us.

If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is removed from your device.

⸻

Data Sharing

CalmTouch does not sell, share, or disclose user data to third parties. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

Subscription purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. Google may collect and process payment and subscription-related information in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy. CalmTouch does not receive or store payment details.

⸻

Third-Party Services

CalmTouch integrates with Google Play solely for subscription and billing management. Any data processed by Google is governed by Google’s own privacy practices.

⸻

Security

CalmTouch relies on your device’s operating system security features (such as device encryption, passcode protection, or biometric authentication) to protect locally stored data. CalmTouch does not have remote access to user data.

⸻

Children’s Privacy

CalmTouch is not designed for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has used CalmTouch and entered personal information, please contact us.

⸻

Your Rights

You retain full control over your data. Since data is stored locally on your device, you may remove all app data at any time by uninstalling the app.

⸻

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected by updating the “Last Updated” date above.

### Data Safety Summary

- Data shared: None
- Data used for tracking: None
- Account creation: Not required
